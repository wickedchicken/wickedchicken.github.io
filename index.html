German for Programmers

Feb 3, 2019 - 13 minutes
After 2 years of learning German I’ve noticed that, for the most part, you can go a long way by mapping foreign concepts to ones that you already know. In particular, I’ve had success mapping aspects of German grammar to programming concepts I use every day. After all, programmers deal with weird grammars all the time, why not take advantage of that skill? Gendered nouns: forward error correction Probably the first difficulty people run into when learning German is memorizing the gender of each noun and properly declining that noun’s articles when used in a sentence.
